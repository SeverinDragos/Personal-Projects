Disney Movies

Since the introduction of Disney films into our society, there has been a sea-change of influence in both the characters and gender roles of people in our society. The films have brought about the shaping of morals, behaviors and characters of not only children, but also adults in todays society. Not surprisingly, many Disney movies follow a tradition of supernatural entities, wishes, and dreams or a combination of all three. All the animations encompass society's worst points and are used as examples of how to surpass the factors that stifle childhood imagination and creativity.
